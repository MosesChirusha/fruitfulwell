Where can we donate for this program?

You can directly donate through our online donation gateway and will receive an American or Canadian Tax receipt from Multiply
Or you can make your donation directly to Well of plenty

How to become a partener of Fruitful-well?

Any organization willing to support this program is almost welcome. To receive the conditions and requirements of parteenership, you can directly send us an email with REQUEST FOR PARTERNERSHIP as subject

Why should I donate for Fruitful-well?

Your gift, combined with other support, will help us to solve the most critical problems in developing countries which include hunger, malnutrition, lack of clean water and creating sustainable business opportunities for them.
Your support and contributions will enable us to meet our goals and improve conditions. Your generous donation will fund our mission.

How much of my donation will go toward programs?

More than 80% of your donation will go directly to the program and the remaining will cover administration fees.

Chicken Farmed For Meat and manure production

Drip Irrigation system

With countries facing rain shortage, the use of drip irrigation technologies help farmers to make more crops with just a small quantity of water.
